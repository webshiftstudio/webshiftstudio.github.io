Club Nights

Senior Membership Club Nights

Every Monday, for our Monday Club Members from 7.30 to 10.00pm in The Sweyne School Sports Hall, Rayleigh 

and

Every Tuesday for our League Players 7.00 to 9.30pm, in The David Cox Sports Hall, at The Fitzwimarc School, Rayleigh. 

Play is on 4 Courts at both venues with Feather Shuttles.

All Senior Club nights are organised by the running of a peg-board  that requires Members to add their peg upon arrival and remove them when leaving.

Junior Club Nights

Juniors, Beginners and Improvers

Our Junior Badminton Academy runs Coaching sessions for Junior/Senior School pupils of all abilities. Our aim is to develop our juniors through coaching with the ultimate aim of integrating them into our adult club.

We are always keen to welcome new players.

These Academy Coaching sessions are run by two of our Badminton England Qualified Coaches Marie Baker & Angela McDaniel, helped by Chloe Baker, and are held early Monday Evenings :

For further details contact Marie Baker:

Email:   thebakerz@sky.com

Out of Hours School Badminton Club

The Fitzwimarc School run an “after school” Badminton Club during term times on Monday & Friday Evenings 6.00 to 7.30pm in the David Cox Sports Hall at the School.  This  is run for Local Senior School pupils who are interested in playing socially or wish to be coached.

For further details contact Eric O’Brien who is the Schools Junior Badminton Coaching Officer being a
Badminton England Level 2 Accredited Coach.

Email Eric at:  no1lensman@blueyonder.co.uk
