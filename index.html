Our  FitzWimarc Badminton Club who have Club-nights on Mondays in the Sweyne School Sports Hall and Tuesdays in the David Cox Sports Hall at the FitzWimarc School, is one of the largest and most established Clubs in the County.

With a playing member base of over 40 League Players and 35 Monday Club Players, it is one of the easiest places to get involved in the game.

Our League Players have their Club-nights every Tuesday in the Sports Hall, at the Fitzwimarc School, 7.00 -9.30pm on 4 Courts.

We also run a Monday Club-night, in the Sports Hall at the Sweyne School, 7.30 -10.00pm, where players who do not wish to, or are not quite of the standard for League play, meet and play competitively each week.

Our Club is always on the look out for new and exciting players to join and play in the League Teams and also players to add to the Monday Club Night Membership.

New Members are invited to guest for a couple of weeks before joining permanently, so if you are interested  in joining please contact us on our:

‘CONTACT PAGE’.

Membership Fees

We try to keep our Senior Membership Club fees as competitive as possible.

Our Annual Membership Fee during 2024/25, that runs from 1st September to 31st August each year, is £190 for Tuesday League Playing Members and £175 for Monday Club Players. 

Students, under 18 in full time education, are charged 50% of the Annual fee, and part time students over 18,  75 % of the full fee.

(If required these fees may be paid either in full or in two half’s.)

When Guesting there is a charge of £7 per week for adults and £4 for Students
